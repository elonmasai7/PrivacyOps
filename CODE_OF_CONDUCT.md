# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to an open, safe, and respectful community for contributors across Africa and globally.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on facts.
- Welcome newcomers and different technical backgrounds.
- Prioritize user safety, data protection, and responsible disclosure.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Deliberate misinformation about legal/compliance outcomes.
- Malicious security behavior in community channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or suspend participation for violations.

## Reporting

Report incidents to maintainers through security/contact channels listed in `SECURITY.md`.
